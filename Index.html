Welcome to our app

This is going to be a calorie tracking app

  • AiCalTrack can scan your meals, giving a near exact nutrition label
  • Track the amount of calories, nutrients, proteins, carbs, fats, and sugar in a food item
  • Meal recipes that meet your dietary needs
  • Gives the amount of calories needed to maintain, gain, or lose weight
  • Gives constant reminders for water intake, meal times, and much more

This is AiCalTrack, a calorie tracker based off of OpenAi's ChatGPT. AiCalTrack is dependent on creating an app where simplicity and functionality is first, where people can track their day-to-day meals and strive to reach their goals all the way from weight loss to weight gain.
